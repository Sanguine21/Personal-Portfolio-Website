Who Am I?

I’m Prachi Kumari, a Computer Science student with a growing passion for web designing.

I enjoy turning simple ideas into clean, visually appealing websites that feel modern, organized, and easy to navigate. For me, web design is not just about writing code — it’s about creating thoughtful digital experiences where design, structure, and content work together seamlessly.

I like experimenting with layouts, refining section styling, and improving the overall look and usability of a webpage. Along with front-end fundamentals, I also enjoy content writing and researching ideas that help transform basic pages into well-structured, portfolio-worthy websites. I believe the best way to learn design is by building real projects. Through projects like my Digital Scam Detection project and this personal portfolio, I continue to explore new ideas, strengthen my design thinking, and develop the practical skills needed to start a professional career as a web designer.

Beyond technology, I also enjoy learning languages and currently have beginner-level knowledge of Japanese and German. This reflects my curiosity for learning new things and exploring different perspectives beyond my field of study.
